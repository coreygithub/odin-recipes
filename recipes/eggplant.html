Eggplant Parm

Breaded eggplant parmesan baked with cheese

Description

A simple recipe to make breaded eggplant parmesan in less than an hour.

Ingredients

  • One Eggplant
  • Italian Breadcrumbs
  • Two Eggs
  • Marinara
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

    Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  1. Peel the skin off the eggplant and cut it into thin slices of equal size
  2. Scramble the two eggs and dip the slices into the eggs before covering with the breadcrumbs
  3. Bake the eggplant for 15 minutes at 400 degrees
  4. Flip the slices over and put the desired amount of marinara and parmesan cheese on top
  5. Bake for another 10 minutes and serve
